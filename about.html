About Us

SkyIoT-Tech exists to make industrial and commercial IoT reliable, secure, and easy to operate—so our customers can focus on outcomes, not infrastructure.

Our Mission

We build and deliver IoT platforms and professional services that connect machines, environments, and people. From edge devices to cloud analytics, we provide the foundation for smarter operations, predictive maintenance, and data-driven decisions.

What We Believe

Security and privacy by design—every device and pipeline is built with identity, encryption, and zero-trust principles. Open standards and interoperability—we support MQTT, REST, and common protocols so you are never locked in. Operational excellence—our solutions are built for 24/7 industrial environments with clear SLAs and support.

Who We Work With

We serve manufacturing, energy, logistics, smart buildings, and smart city projects. Our team includes engineers with deep experience in embedded systems, cloud platforms, and OT/IT integration. Whether you need a pilot or a global rollout, we partner with you from design through deployment and beyond.
